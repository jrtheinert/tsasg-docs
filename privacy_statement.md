Privacy Policy for TSASG WordGame
Last Updated: June 20, 2026
TSASG WordGame ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. Our core philosophy is simple: we want you to play our puzzles, not harvest your data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and how it is protected when you use our mobile application.
1. How We Handle Your Data
We operate under a two-tier data model designed to optimize your puzzle experience while keeping your real-world identity completely private:
Guest Mode (Anonymous Telemetry): For players using the free version of the app, we log basic, non-identifiable gameplay events (such as puzzle completion times, win/loss ratios, and which game modes are played most frequently). We use this as an aggregated diagnostic tool to help us balance puzzle difficulty and improve the game. This data is entirely decoupled from your identity; we do not know who you are.
Premium Profiles (Cloud Sync): If you unlock the full archive (subscribe to Premium), we store your game progress, word statistics, and a custom Display Name on our secure servers. We do this for one specific reason: Cross-Device Play and Account Recovery. This ensures that if you pick up a tablet or upgrade to a new phone, your puzzle history moves with you. This backup is tied strictly to an anonymous system token generated by Google Play Billing—it is never linked to an email address, your real name, or an advertising ID.
2. Third-Party Services We Use
To provide essential app functionality, we integrate secure, industry-standard third-party services. These services may collect strictly necessary technical data:
Google Play Services / Google Play Billing: Used for processing app downloads, secure in-app purchases, and collecting basic, anonymized crash logs to help us fix bugs.
RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to validate and manage your active subscriptions and one-time purchases. RevenueCat assigns an anonymous "App User ID" to your device to ensure you retain access to your purchases across device restores. They do not know your name, email, or identity.
For more information on how these partners handle data, please refer to the Google Privacy Policy and the RevenueCat Privacy Policy.
3. Children's Privacy
Our game is an educational vocabulary and puzzle tool suitable for all ages, but we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Because we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data of children to delete.
4. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy periodically as we add new features. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
